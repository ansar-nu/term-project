Natural heritage around Shymkent

In the heart of Central Asia, South Kazakhstan unfolds a spectacular panorama of natural wonders and cultural treasures. From the majestic peaks of the mountains to the vast steppes that stretch as far as the eye can see, this region is a testament to the breathtaking beauty of untouched landscapes. Get ready to embark on an adventure where every landscape tells a story, and every monument echoes the past.

Top Places to spot the best of nature:

  1. Sayram-Ugam
  2. Akmeshit Cave
  3. Arystan-bab Mausoleum

General Information

  • It is the third-most populous city in Kazakhstan, behind Almaty & Astana.
  • A littele historic background: Shymkent grew as a market center for trade between Turkic nomads and the settled Sogdians. The name of the city of Chimkent comes from the words Chim- sod and Kent- city.
  • My hometown is located close to the border with Uzbekistan so it is really convinient to travel to Tashkent from here.
  • We are very famous for our warm weather and welcoming attitude of locals. You will never leave the house hungry or thirsty in Shymkent.

Some Sightseeings

One of the best gathering places for friends, families, and relatives: Arbat, Dendrosayabak, Fontan.

Places to eat in Shymkent:

  • Coffeemolka: a famous coffeeshop. I used to work there.
  • Mizam: they cook central asian cuisine. My high school teachers enjoyed going there.
  • Korizza: another well-known coffee shop in the city. They have really great drinks.
